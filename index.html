Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is P.E.B? ‚ñº

P.E.B is a small project dedicated to creating AI discord bots. The project inspire by the fandom named Fundamental Paper Education by Kaatie A3DGhost

Is P.E.B free to use? ‚ñº

Yes! All P.E.B bots are completely free to use. Simply invite them to your Discord server and start creating amazing roleplay experiences.

How do I add a bot to my server? ‚ñº

Click the "Add Bot" button on any bot card. This will redirect you to Discord's authorization page where you can select your server and permissions.

Technical Questions

What permissions do the bots need? ‚ñº

Our bots require: Send Messages, Read Message History, Embed Links, Manage Channels, and React to Messages. Each bot specifies required permissions during invitation.

How does the dialogue system work? ‚ñº

Simply mention or reply to one of our bots in Discord. The bot will analyze your message and respond with character-appropriate dialogue.
